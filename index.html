Personal by default

Your diary stays yours.

Jiak’s first version is a private diary, not a social network. Your check-ins, notes, photos, collections, labels and Snapshots are visible only to you.

  • No public profile or public feed
  • No sharing links or visibility toggles
  • No website cookies or analytics
  • Delete memories—or your whole account—when you choose
